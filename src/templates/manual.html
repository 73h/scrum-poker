Start a poker session

You start a poker session by entering a username and selecting your desired card set.

username

The username must contain at least two characters.

cards

You can choose between the following card sets. You cannot change the cards during the poker round.

session password (optional)

You can set a session password to exclude uninvited players. The password is optional.

Invite players and play

To invite other players, you can simply copy the current address from your browser and send it to your players. Do not forget to tell them the password as well, if you have assigned one.

If you share your screen with your players, for example in a zoom session, then you can also use the qrcode button. The players can then easily participate via phone.

As soon as all players are online, the game can start. The first round of poker is already underway. Everyone can vote now.

When everyone has voted, you can uncover the results by clicking the uncover button.

For a new vote, you should press the new vote button.

And so on...

Have fun!
